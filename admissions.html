Admissions

Join our vibrant community where academic excellence meets character development.

Admission Requirements

Primary School

  • Birth Certificate
  • Recent Report Card
  • Completed Application Form

Junior High School

  • Birth Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Completed Application Form

How to Apply

  1. Download the application form here.
  2. Fill out the form and attach the required documents.
  3. Submit the form in person or via email to admissions@regis.edu.gh.
  4. Pay the application fee and await confirmation.

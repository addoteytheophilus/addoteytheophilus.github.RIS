About Us

Discover the history, mission, and values that define REGI'S International School.

Our History

REGI'S International School was founded in 1990 with a mission to provide quality education to students in Nsakina and beyond. Over the years, we have grown into a community of excellence, fostering academic and personal growth for all our students.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards, fostering trust and honesty in all our interactions.

Excellence

We strive for outstanding performance, consistently aiming for the best in everything we do.

Collaboration

We believe in teamwork and value diverse perspectives to achieve common goals.

Respect

We treat everyone with dignity, kindness, and fairness, nurturing a positive community.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and forward-thinking to drive progress and deliver solutions.
